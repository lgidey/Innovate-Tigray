Who we are

Innovate Tigray is a nonprofit organization which focuses on providing quality education to talented high school students.
We provide college application workshops, networking opportunities, skill trainings, professional and
academic mentorship to talented high school students from Tigray region in northern Ethiopia.

Our Mission

We work to create a competitive and collaborative environment wherein out-performers would get motivated.
We build a bridge among communities and would be able to exchange educational and cultural experiences.
We promote and advance students’ invention and creativity aspirations.
Our programs include preparing educational workshops, culture-sharing, networking events, and holding fundraising
events in order provide educational and financial assistance to exceptionally talented students.
